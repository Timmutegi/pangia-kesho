FAQS

1. What is Imarisha Afya?

Imarisha Afya is an annual family Health Cover offered by Bliss Healthcare Ltd in partnership with Bluewave Insurance Agency Ltd. The Health Cover offers both Inpatient, Outpatient, Dental, Optical and Maternity services.


2. How do I buy Imarisha Afya?

You will enrol by clicking Imarisha Afya link on Bluewave’s website. The link directs you to a page where you will key in your details and pay the premiums. A message notifying you of your registration will be sent to your mobile number. You can also register by downloading the Imarisha Afya app from Playstore, submit your details and pay the premiums. An e-card will be issued to you upon successful registration.


3. How much does Imarisha Afya cost?

Imarisha Afya costs only Kshs 20,000 for both Inpatient and Outpatient services. If Outpatient Cover only, the cost is Kshs 15,000.


4. Do I need to pay the full amount at once or can I pay bit by bit?

For the Health Cover to be active, you need to pay the full amount. Kshs 20,000 for both Inpatient and Outpatient or Kshs 15,000 for Outpatient only.


5. Is there a Loan facility available?

Yes! Bluewave insurance agency has partnered with Microfinance and micro lenders to assist in premium financing for Imarisha Afya. Please call us on 0739599914 for more details.


6. Who is eligible for Imarisha Afya?

All Kenyan citizens and residents who are of ages 18-80 are eligible for Imarisha Afya. The spouses must be of ages 18-80 while the children should be of ages between 0-18. Children of ages 18 years up to 25 years can be included (evidence of schooling must be provided).


7. Which services are covered under Imarisha Afya?

Inpatient and Outpatient services as per the table below:

INPATIENT DEPARTMENT (Up to KES 500,000) OUTPATIENT DEPARTMENT (Up to KES 75,000)
  • Dental and Ophthalmology KES 75,000
  • Congenital Conditions sub-limit of KES 75,000
  • Chronic and Psychiatric Conditions sub-limit of KES 200,000
  • Maternity KES 75,000 for Principal Member and Spouse
  • Basic Dental KES 10,000
  • Basic Optical KES 10,000. Frames and lenses sub-limit of KES 6,000
  • Physiotherapy maximum 5 sessions within Outpatient cover.

8. How many people are covered in the Imarisha Afya Health Cover?

Imarisha Afya covers a family of 5 members, who are the Principal Member, a spouse and three children.


9. How much will I pay if I do not have any children or spouse?

The cost will remain the same.


10. What if I have an additional family member?

There is an extra fee of KES 6,000 payable for every additional family member.


11. Is there a Waiting Period for Imarisha Afya?

There is a one month waiting Period for Imarisha Afya.


12. Does Imarisha Afya accumulate if I do not fall sick?

Imarisha Afya does not accumulate or roll over to the next year if you do not use the services.


13. Can foreign residents buy Imarisha Afya?

Imarisha Afya is available to only Kenyan citizens and residents as Bliss Healthcare, Lifecare and Medicross Healthcare clinics are only in Kenya currently.


14. Where are the Bliss Healthcare clinics located?

The clinics are located countrywide in Nairobi, Coast, Central, Rift Valley, Nyanza and Western regions. See poster for detailed clinics.


15. Bliss healthcare are clinics, what happens if I require surgery or admission?

Bliss healthcare has partnered with various healthcare providers who have inpatient facilities should one require surgery or admission.


16. What if the cost of surgery is more than the Inpatient limit?

Imarisha Afya can only cater for medical services within the Inpatient limit, should the bill exceed the customer will settle the outstanding amount (through other medical covers or cash).


17. Do I have to be registered for NHIF?

No, you do not have to be registered for NHIF to buy Imarisha Afya. However, you can still have NHIF and Imarisha Afya.


18. Are there any exclusions?

The following exclusions will hold:

  • Aphrodisiacs
  • Hormonal replacement therapy
  • Fertility treatment
  • Chiropractors, Acupuncturist, Herbalist
  • External and Internal prosthesis
  • Cosmetic Surgery and Beauty treatment and any operations, treatments and or procedures of own choice for purely cosmetic purposes or obesity, and any complications that may arise from such operations, treatment and or procedures.
  • Treatment of impotence.
  • Sickness or complications arising from non-compliance to prescribed treatment by an empanelled doctor.
  • Intentional self-injury, attempted suicide, drug addiction, intoxication, drunkenness
  • Naval military and Air force operations
  • Compensation for pain and suffering from loss of income or claims for damages arising from any illegal or criminal act.
  • Last Expense
  • Search and rescue
  • Air rescue and evacuation
  • Evacuation and ambulatory services unless ambulatory services for hospital to hospital transfers
  • Maternity and related complications not covered for dependants. Maternity only covers the principal member, spouse and new-born.

19. How many times can I go to the hospital in a month?

You can go to the hospital as long as your cover benefit is not exceeded.


20. Is maternity included in the cover?

Yes, maternity is included within the Inpatient package and capped at KShs. 75,000 per year.


21. How can I be assisted if I have a complaint or question?

You can call 0739599914 any time of the day or night or email info@bluewaveinsurance.co for assistance.
